# Creating an Account

Getting started with CreditClaw takes just a few minutes. This guide walks you through the sign-up process and what to expect once you're in.

## Signing Up

Visit [creditclaw.com](/) and enter your email address in the hero section. You have two options:

- **Join the waitlist** — Get notified when your account is ready
- **Try it now** — Jump straight into the onboarding flow

If you choose to try it now, you'll be taken to the onboarding wizard where you can set everything up in one go.

## Authentication

CreditClaw uses Firebase Authentication. You can sign in with:

- **Email and password**
- **Google account**

Once authenticated, you'll land on the main <a href="/app" target="_blank">dashboard</a>.

## The Onboarding Wizard

When you first sign in, the onboarding wizard walks you through the essential setup steps:

1. **Choose your path** — Select whether you're setting up a bot for purchasing, selling, or both
2. **Connect a bot** — Link your AI agent using a pairing code
3. **Add a payment method** — Set up a wallet or link a card
4. **Set spending limits** — Configure per-transaction, daily, and monthly caps
5. **Choose approval mode** — Decide whether purchases need your approval
6. **Configure categories** — Block or allow specific merchant categories
7. **Add special instructions** — Any additional rules for your bot

You can skip the wizard and configure these settings later from the dashboard, but completing it upfront ensures your bot is ready to operate safely from the start.

## What Happens After Signup

Once you've completed signup (with or without the wizard), you'll have access to the full <a href="/app" target="_blank">dashboard</a> where you can:

- View and manage your connected bots
- Create and fund wallets
- Monitor transactions and activity
- Set up checkout pages and invoices for selling
- Configure spending controls and guardrails
